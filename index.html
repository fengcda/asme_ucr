About us

Welcome To ASME at UCR

We are the American Society of Mechanical Engineers. Our goal is to provide various opportunities to network, acquire professional skills, and gain real-world experience.

As a club, we hope to serve, network, and professionally develop the engineers of UC Riverside, as well as provide them with the skills and tools needed to be lifelong learners and prepare for and attain careers in the real world.

Why join ASME?

Collaboration

ASME fosters collaboration between students of all disciplines. Through our technical projects, members must collectively apply their skills to create an end product.

Skill Development

ASME hosts frequent professional development events, such as resume and interview workshops, company tours, and more.

Why join ASME?

Family

As a social organization, ASME places great emphasis on cultivating friendship and camaraderie. Come check us out at our socials: dorm dinners, beach bonfires, KBBQ runs, study sessions, and more!
